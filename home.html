What I Do:

I develop software with a commitment to solutions-oriented activities, bringing my experience to bear from conception through implementation.

I engage in effective leadership-by-example through service to my team, using excellent communication and presentation skills to facilitate success.

My Journey

I graduated from Biola University in La Mirada, California with high honors (3.98 GPA) for a BS in Organizational Leadership. I have augmented my degree with Certification as a Full Stack JavaScript Developer from Team Treehouse and other development-oriented courses.

I began my career as a software developer and solutions implementer, following a path of employee, contractor, consultant, manager, entrepreneur and IT project manager. After running that course, I discovered that software development was what gave me the greatest sense of productivity and contribution to the enhancement of the lives of the people for whom I do the work.

It is what motivated me to return to "my roots" a few years back.
